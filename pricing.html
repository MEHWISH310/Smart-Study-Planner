Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! Our Student plan is already discounted for students. If you need additional assistance, please contact our support team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and in some regions, bank transfers.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to your plan until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all annual subscriptions. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time.
