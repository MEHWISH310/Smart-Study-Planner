My Project Story

Project Mission

Smart Study Planner was created as a personal project to address the challenges students face in managing their academic workload effectively. As a student myself, I understood the struggle of balancing multiple subjects, assignments, and deadlines.


My mission is to empower students of all ages and educational levels to take control of their learning journey through intuitive tools that simplify task management, enhance productivity, and promote healthy study habits.


I believe that effective time management and organization are fundamental skills that contribute not only to academic success but also to personal growth and lifelong learning.
