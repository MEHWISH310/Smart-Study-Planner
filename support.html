Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Smart Study Planner.

How do I create a study schedule?

To create a study schedule, go to the Dashboard and click on "Create Schedule." You can then add your courses, set study times, and customize your schedule based on your preferences and availability.

Can I sync my calendar with Smart Study Planner?

Yes, you can sync with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook. Go to Settings > Calendar Integration to connect your preferred calendar service.

How do I track my study progress?

Use the Progress section to view your study statistics. You can track time spent studying, completed tasks, and goal achievements. The dashboard also provides visual charts of your progress over time.

Is my data secure with Smart Study Planner?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal data with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I use Smart Study Planner on multiple devices?

Yes, your account syncs across all your devices. You can access your study plans from your computer, tablet, or smartphone using our web app or mobile applications.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a password reset link to your registered email address. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team.
