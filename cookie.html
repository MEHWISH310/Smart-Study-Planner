Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Smart Study Planner uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website and application.

Our Use of Cookies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

How We Use Cookies

Smart Study Planner uses cookies for several purposes, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests.

Types of Cookies We Use

The table below explains the types of cookies we use and why we use them:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Authentication Cookies To identify you when you visit our website and as you navigate our website Session / Persistent
Preferences Cookies To store information about your preferences and to personalize the website for you Persistent (1 year)
Security Cookies As a security measure to protect user accounts Session
Analytics Cookies To help us understand how visitors interact with our website Persistent (2 years)
Advertising Cookies To make advertising messages more relevant to you Persistent (1 year)

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the service, deliver advertisements on and through the service, and so on.

These third-party services may include:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Google AdWords: For advertising and remarketing purposes
  • Social Media Platforms: For social media integration and sharing
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing

Note: We do not have control over third-party cookies. You should check the respective privacy policies of these third-party providers for more detailed information on their cookies.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have several options to control or limit how we and our partners use cookies:

  • Browser Controls: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.
  • Opt-Out Tools: You can opt out of certain types of cookies, particularly those used for targeted advertising, through industry opt-out tools.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites you do not want to have your online activities tracked.

Your current cookie preferences: Not set

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your web browser settings. The "Help" feature on most browsers will tell you how to:

  • Prevent your browser from accepting new cookies
  • Have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie
  • Disable cookies altogether
  • Delete cookies that have already been set

If you'd like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.
